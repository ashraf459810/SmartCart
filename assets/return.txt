	•	No longer want an item (because it is the wrong size or color).
	•	Personalized items and custom-made items, for example curtains.
	•	Cosmetics and personal care products, undergarments, swimwear, bikinis, books, magazines, copyable software, digital programs and digital keycodes, DVD, CD and cassettes, and stationary consumables like (toner, cartridge, tape, etc.)

	•	Home appliances such as refrigerators, washing machines and televisions.

You can have the refund by submitting the damage report.
